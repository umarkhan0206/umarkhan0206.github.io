About

I'm a Data Scientist and ML Engineer completing my MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Leeds. My work spans financial risk intelligence, clinical NLP, and biomedical image analysis.

What sets me apart is a consistent commitment to honest evaluation. I don't just build until a model looks good on a test split. I stress-test assumptions before writing code, document failures openly, and report what I actually find. That approach produced Redline (87.1% accuracy, 16/16 insolvent cases recalled) and MedIntent (18% to 92.3% real-world reliability across 12 LIME-diagnosed iterations).
